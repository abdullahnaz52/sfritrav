Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: January 15, 2025 · Effective: January 15, 2025

Welcome to SfriTrav ("we", "our", "us"), operated from India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit sfritrav.com ("the Website"). By using our Website, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically collected: When you visit, we automatically collect IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, time on site, and referring URLs via analytics tools. We use Google Analytics (GA4) for this.

Voluntarily provided: If you subscribe to our newsletter or submit a contact form, we collect your name and email address (and optionally, phone number). This data is processed by Formspree.

Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience, analyze traffic, and serve relevant advertisements. See our Cookie Policy section below.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the Website
  • To send newsletters you've subscribed to (with your consent)
  • To respond to contact form inquiries
  • To display personalized advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze traffic and user behavior via Google Analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to show ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

We also participate in the Google AdSense program. Our website has not been reviewed or approved by any official Google entity. AdSense revenue does not affect our editorial independence.

4. Cookies Policy

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics GA4 to understand how visitors use the site. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense/DoubleClick for personalized ads. Can be opted out via Google's ad settings.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your cookie consent choice and display preferences.

We display a cookie consent banner to users in GDPR-applicable regions. Users can accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice is stored in your browser's localStorage.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third parties, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Formspree: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy — processes form submissions
  • Unsplash: Images from unsplash.com per their privacy policy
  • Google Fonts: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Affiliate Links

Some links on SfriTrav may be affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you make a purchase through them, at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial content. We only recommend products and services we genuinely believe in.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / General)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw consent: For newsletter subscriptions, unsubscribe anytime via the link in any email

To exercise your rights, email: sfritrav_2026@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form data is retained for 12 months, then deleted. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' data retention settings (default 26 months).

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including HTTPS (SSL), Content Security Policy headers, rate limiting, input sanitization, and secure form processing via Formspree. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us to remove it.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Website after changes constitutes acceptance.

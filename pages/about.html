✈️ Our Story

Born in the valleys of Kashmir, built for every traveller who believes the journey is as sacred as the destination.

Who We Are

SfriTrav (सफ़र + Travel) is an independent travel and lifestyle blog headquartered in Srinagar, Kashmir, India. We are passionate travellers, writers, and photographers who believe that authentic stories — not just tourist tips — transform journeys.

Our focus is on the destinations closest to our hearts: the majestic valleys of Kashmir, the sacred paths of Hajj and Umrah, the ancient streets of India's cultural capitals, and the sensory world of Arabic and Indian perfumery.

We write for the Indian traveller — budget-conscious, spiritually aware, culture-hungry, and always searching for the road less taken.

What We Cover

🏔️ Kashmir Travel: Dal Lake, Gulmarg skiing, Pahalgam meadows, Sonamarg glaciers, Doodhpathri, and the hidden treks that most tourists never find.

🕌 Hajj & Umrah: Complete pilgrimage guides for Indian Muslims — Nusuk app tutorials, visa processes, budget breakdowns, packing lists, and the spiritual preparation that matters most.

🌹 Perfumes & Attars: Deep-dives into Arabic oud, Indian ittar, non-alcoholic fragrances from Madinah's souks, and luxury niche perfumes available in India — in partnership with SafarOoh Shop.

🇮🇳 India Travel: Varanasi ghats, Rajasthan forts, Kerala backwaters, Himalayan road trips, and everything in between.

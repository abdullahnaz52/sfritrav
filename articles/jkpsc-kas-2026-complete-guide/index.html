Jkpsc Kas 2026 Complete Guide

📅 2026-04-18 ⏱ 9 min read ✍️ SfriTrav Editorial Team

The Jammu & Kashmir Public Service Commission (JKPSC) conducts the KAS (Kashmir Administrative Service) Combined Competitive Examination for appointment to J&K Civil Services. It is the state-level equivalent of UPSC and follows a similar three-stage pattern.

Exam Pattern

The JKPSC KAS exam has three stages: Preliminary Exam (Objective), Mains (Descriptive), and Viva-voce. The Preliminary has two papers — General Studies (200 marks) and Mental Ability/Reasoning. Mains has six papers including Essay, General Studies I/II/III, and one Optional subject.

J&K Specific Topics (High Weightage)

  • History and Culture of J&K — Dogra Period, Kashmir under Mughals, Sheikh Abdullah era
  • Geography of J&K — Chenab, Jhelum, Indus basins; glaciers, Pir Panjal range
  • J&K Economy — Horticulture, tourism, handicrafts (Pashmina, Kashmiri carpets, Khatamband)
  • J&K Administration — Article 370 abrogation (2019), UT status, Delimitation Commission
  • J&K Current Affairs — LG Administration, new district formation, development projects

Key Resources

  • J&K at a Glance (official J&K government publication)
  • JKPSC previous years' question papers (available at jkpsc.nic.in)
  • Kashmir Life Magazine for J&K current affairs
  • Standard UPSC books for GS (M Laxmikant, Spectrum, NCERT)

Official portal: jkpsc.nic.in — always check here for notification, admit card download and result links.

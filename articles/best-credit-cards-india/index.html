Best Credit Cards India

📅 2026-04-18 ⏱ 9 min read ✍️ SfriTrav Editorial Team

The right credit card in India can save you ₹15,000–₹50,000 per year through rewards, cashback and lounge access. The wrong one costs you interest at 36–42% annually. Here is exactly what to choose based on your spending pattern.

Best Overall: HDFC Regalia Gold

5X reward points on dining and international spends. 4 complimentary lounge visits per quarter (16/year). Annual fee ₹2,500 — waived on ₹4L annual spend. Best for: frequent travelers and high spenders.

Best Cashback: Axis Bank Ace Credit Card

5% cashback on bill payments via Google Pay. 4% on Swiggy, Zomato and Ola. 1.5% on everything else. Annual fee ₹499 (waived on ₹2L spend). Best for: everyday spenders who prefer cashback over points.

Best No-Fee Card: SBI SimplyCLICK

10X rewards on Amazon, BookMyShow, Cleartrip, Lenskart. Annual fee ₹499 but reversed if you spend ₹1L/year. Best for: online shoppers who want no-cost rewards.

Best Premium: HDFC Infinia Metal

By-invitation only. Unlimited lounge access worldwide. 5 reward points per ₹150. Golf privileges. Best for: those spending ₹8L+ annually who want maximum lifestyle benefits.

How to Use Credit Cards Without Going Into Debt

One rule: always pay the full statement balance every month. Never pay just the minimum due — the remaining balance attracts 3–3.5% per month (42% per year). Set up auto-pay for the full amount. Treat your credit card as a debit card that gives you rewards — not as borrowed money.
